With over three decades of immersion in Hindustani Classical Music, Chandrabali Rudra has become one of the most respected voices in the classical vocal tradition. Her journey began at the feet of legendary masters, and today she carries that torch forward — lighting the path for hundreds of aspiring musicians.

Her teaching philosophy bridges the sacred rigor of classical raga training with an intuitive understanding of each student's unique musical soul. Under her guidance, ancient art forms become alive, accessible, and profoundly transformative.

The Journey

A Life Devoted
to Music

1990

Musical Awakening

Began formal training in Hindustani Classical Vocals under the tutelage of her Guruji — embarking on a lifelong devotion to the world of ragas, talas, and the sacred oral tradition.

1998

First Grand Recital

Delivered her debut major public performance to a standing ovation. The concert received critical acclaim and established her as a rising star in classical music circles.

2005

Academy Founded

Established the Chandrabali Music Academy — a sacred space for aspiring musicians to discover their voice and connect deeply with India's rich classical traditions.

2012

National Recognition

Received the prestigious National Music Award for outstanding contribution to classical vocal arts, cementing her status as a guardian of India's musical heritage.

2018

Going Global

Extended teaching to students across 15+ countries through live online platforms — bringing the purity and depth of Hindustani classical music to every corner of the world.

2024

Legacy Continues

500+ students trained, 50+ stage performances, and an unwavering commitment to passing the torch of classical music to the next generation — the journey never ends.
